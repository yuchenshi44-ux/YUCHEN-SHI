Pediatric Dentistry

We specialize in providing gentle dental care for children from infancy through adolescence. Our goal is to create positive experiences that help children develop good oral hygiene habits and maintain healthy smiles for life.

Our pediatric services include:

  • Routine check-ups and cleanings
  • Fluoride treatments and sealants
  • Tooth-colored fillings
  • Behavior management techniques for anxious children
  • Orthodontic evaluations

Diagnostic Services

Accurate diagnosis is the foundation of effective dental care. We use advanced technology to identify dental issues early, when they're easiest to treat.

Our diagnostic services include:

  • Digital X-rays (with 80% less radiation than traditional X-rays)
  • Intraoral cameras for detailed examination
  • Comprehensive oral examinations
  • Oral cancer screenings
  • 3D imaging for complex cases

Preventive Care

Preventing dental problems before they start is key to maintaining optimal oral health. Our preventive services help you keep your natural teeth healthy for a lifetime.

Our preventive services include:

  • Regular dental check-ups and cleanings
  • Fluoride treatments
  • Dental sealants
  • Oral hygiene education
  • Custom mouthguards for sports
